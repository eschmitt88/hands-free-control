# Visual Evoked Potential
## Definition
An EEG response evoked by a visual stimulus, decoded to infer visual attention or intent; it is the basis of SSVEP and NextMind-style selection.
## Why it matters here
It underlies the most usable consumer BCI selection paradigms, but as an EEG signal it sits in the project's parked, low-priority track behind voice and gaze.
## Connections
- [[ssvep]] — the steady-state flicker-locked application of this evoked response
