# Event-related Potential
## Definition
A time-locked EEG deflection following a stimulus, such as the P300, detected via averaging across many epochs.
## Why it matters here
Its reliance on epoch averaging explains EEG's inherent selection latency, reinforcing why EEG remains parked and low-priority relative to the project's voice and gaze channels.
