# Head-pose Pointing
## Definition
Mapping camera-captured head or facial-feature motion to cursor position as a hands-free pointing channel.
## Why it matters here
It is a robust alternative or complement to gaze for coarse pointing, and a proven baseline the multimodal gaze-plus-voice thesis must beat or absorb.
## Connections
- [[feature-tracking]] — the mechanism that turns head motion into a trackable signal
- [[camera-mouse]] — the tool class that popularized head-pose pointing
- [[dwell-click]] — the usual selection method paired with head pointing
- [[gaze-cursor-positioning]] — the competing camera-driven pointing channel
