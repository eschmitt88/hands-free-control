# BCI Illiteracy
## Definition
The phenomenon in which roughly half of users cannot produce usable control signals with a given BCI paradigm.
## Why it matters here
A control channel that fails for half its users cannot be the project's primary modality, which is a central reason EEG stays parked and low-priority behind voice and gaze.
## Connections
- [[inter-subject-variability]] — the underlying EEG variability that produces non-responders
- [[consumer-bci-hardware]] — cheap few-channel headsets exhibit illiteracy most acutely
