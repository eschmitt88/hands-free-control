# Dwell Click
## Definition
Clicking by holding the pointer within a small radius for a set dwell time, avoiding an explicit manual click.
## Why it matters here
It is the default selection primitive for camera and gaze pointing, but its latency and false triggers are exactly what voice-based selection can replace.
