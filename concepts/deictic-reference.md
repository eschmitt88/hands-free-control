# Deictic Reference
## Definition
Pointing-word commands such as "that" or "there" whose referent is supplied by a concurrent spatial modality (gaze or pointing) rather than by the words themselves.
## Why it matters here
Deixis is the natural grammar for gaze-plus-voice control: users can say "open that" while looking, letting the system resolve the target from gaze instead of demanding verbose descriptions.
## Connections
- [[mutual-disambiguation]] — deictic words are meaningless until the spatial modality disambiguates them
- [[pronoun-disambiguation]] — resolving the deictic referent is a disambiguation task
- [[gaze-context-grounding]] — gaze supplies the anchor that the deictic term points to
