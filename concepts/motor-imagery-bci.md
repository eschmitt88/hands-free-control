# Motor-imagery BCI
## Definition
Decoding imagined limb movement from sensorimotor EEG; the paradigm suffers high inter-subject variability and needs well-placed central electrodes.
## Why it matters here
Its fragility and per-user calibration burden are part of why EEG is parked and low-priority here; it is noted as a paradigm to watch rather than one to build against now.

- [[inter-subject-variability]] — the dominant limitation that undermines motor-imagery reliability
