# Inter-subject Variability
## Definition
Large between- and within-subject differences in EEG that degrade cross-session and cross-user generalization of BCI decoders.
## Why it matters here
It forces per-user, per-session calibration that is impractical for everyday desktop control, reinforcing the project's parked, low-priority stance on EEG.
## Connections
- [[motor-imagery-bci]] — the paradigm most degraded by this variability
- [[bci-illiteracy]] — variability at the extreme yields users with no usable signal
