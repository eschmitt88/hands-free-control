# Interaction-based Calibration
## Definition
Self-calibrating the gaze-to-screen mapping from natural clicks and interactions, rather than from an explicit calibration grid.
## Why it matters here
It removes an upfront setup burden, letting gaze pointing improve silently as the user works, which matters for adoption of hands-free control.
