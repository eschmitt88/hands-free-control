# P300 Speller
## Definition
An ERP-based BCI that uses the P300 oddball response to attended stimuli in a flashing grid for target selection, requiring trial averaging to detect the response.
## Why it matters here
It is a reference point for how slow and averaging-dependent EEG selection is; since EEG is parked here, the P300 speller mainly illustrates why voice and gaze are the priority channels.

- [[event-related-potential]] — the P300 is the ERP this speller detects
