# Gaze Estimation Benchmark
## Definition
A standardized dataset, split, and evaluation protocol that makes gaze-estimation results comparable across methods.
## Why it matters here
Benchmarks let the project pick a gaze backbone on evidence rather than vendor claims, grounding the choice of pointing model.
