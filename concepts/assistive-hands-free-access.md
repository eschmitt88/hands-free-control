# Assistive Hands-free Access
## Definition
Hands-free computer access for motor-impaired users, combining gaze and speech as a substitute for the mouse and keyboard.
## Why it matters here
It is the core motivation for the project: users who cannot use their hands are the primary beneficiaries, and their needs set the reliability and effort bar for the gaze-plus-voice design.
