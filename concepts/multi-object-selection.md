# Multi-object Selection
## Definition
Selecting several objects at once, decomposed into a mode-switch (enter multi-select) followed by per-object subselection.
## Why it matters here
It extends hands-free control beyond single picks to batch operations, and its mode-switch-then-select structure maps naturally onto a voice trigger plus repeated gaze picks.
## Connections
- [[mode-switching]] — entering multi-select is a mode switch best driven by a discrete voice command
