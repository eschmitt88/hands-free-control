# Camera Mouse
## Definition
The class of free webcam mouse-replacement tools that track the head or face to move the cursor for motor-disabled users.
## Why it matters here
It is the prior art and accessibility baseline this project extends, showing what hands-free pointing already delivers and where voice can improve it.

- [[dwell-click]] — the selection method they pair with head pointing
