# Consumer BCI Hardware
## Definition
Low-cost commercial EEG headsets — Muse, Emotiv, OpenBCI, NextMind — characterized by few channels, high variability, and product-longevity risk.
## Why it matters here
These are the only affordable EEG options a real user would own, and their limitations plus discontinuation risk (e.g. NextMind) are a concrete reason EEG remains parked and low-priority here.
