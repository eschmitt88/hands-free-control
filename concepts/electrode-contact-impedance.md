# Electrode Contact Impedance
## Definition
The skin-electrode electrical resistance; dry electrodes reach over 100 kOhm versus under 10 kOhm for wet, attenuating low frequency bands and amplifying noise.
## Why it matters here
It is the concrete physical reason dry consumer EEG is noisy, and thus part of the technical case for keeping EEG parked and low-priority in this project.
