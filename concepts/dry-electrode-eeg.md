# Dry-electrode EEG
## Definition
Gel-free EEG electrodes offering faster setup but suffering over 100 kOhm contact impedance, more motion and line noise, and lower SNR than wet electrodes.
## Why it matters here
Dry electrodes are the only realistic form factor for daily hands-free use, yet their noise penalty is part of why EEG stays parked here as a future rather than current channel.
