# Non-invasive BCI
## Definition
A BCI that uses externally worn scalp EEG rather than implanted electrodes, trading lower risk for lower signal quality.
## Why it matters here
Only non-invasive BCI is remotely deployable for everyday desktop users, but its weak signal keeps it in the project's parked, low-priority track behind voice and gaze.
