# Target Expansion
## Definition
Dynamically magnifying the gaze-targeted object to reduce pointing error on small targets.
## Why it matters here
It boosts effective target size for imprecise gaze, improving selection reliability for motor-impaired users who cannot fall back on fine manual pointing.
