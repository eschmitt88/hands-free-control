# ETH-XGaze (captured abstract + metadata)

**Full Title:** ETH-XGaze: A Large Scale Dataset for Gaze Estimation under
Extreme Head Pose and Gaze Variation

**Authors:** Xucong Zhang, Seonwook Park, Thabo Beeler, Derek Bradley,
Siyu Tang, Otmar Hilliges

**Institution:** ETH Zurich (with Disney Research / Google authors among the
co-authors, e.g. Beeler, Bradley).

**Year:** 2020

**Venue:** ECCV 2020 (Spotlight)

**Abstract:** Introduces a benchmark dataset providing "over one million
high-resolution images of varying gaze under extreme head poses." Addresses gaps
in existing datasets by offering comprehensive head-pose and gaze-angle
variations with standardized evaluation protocols.

**Dataset Details (ETH-XGaze):**
- Scale: Over 1 million high-resolution images
- Participants: 110 subjects
- Capture Setup: 18 digital SLR cameras with calibrated ground-truth recording
- Conditions: Adjustable illumination environments
- Coverage: Extreme head poses and gaze variations

**Standardized Benchmark:** Unified experimental protocols and evaluation
metrics to improve consistency across gaze-estimation research. A baseline CNN
(ResNet-family) is provided.

**Code/Dataset Release:** https://ait.ethz.ch/projects/2020/ETH-XGaze
