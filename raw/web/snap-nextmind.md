# Welcome NextMind (Snap acquisition announcement)

## What was fetched
Snap's 2022 announcement that NextMind - a Paris-based non-invasive BCI company -
joined Snap to drive long-term AR research within Snap Lab. Team continues in Paris.

Technology described: monitors neural signals to interpret user intent for
hands-free interaction with computers and AR/VR wearables. Snap clarifies it does
not "read" thoughts or send signals into the brain; users act on a virtual
interface by focusing (e.g., activate a button by concentrating on it) - i.e., a
visual-evoked-potential (VEP) attention-decoding mechanism.

## External context (domain knowledge, clearly marked, unverified from page)
NextMind sold a ~$399 dry-electrode developer kit before the acquisition that
decoded visual attention (VEP) from occipital electrodes to drive cursor/
selection. After the Snap acquisition the standalone hardware and developer
portal were discontinued - a cautionary tale about consumer-BCI product
longevity when a startup is acqui-hired.
