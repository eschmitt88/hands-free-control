Closed-loop head pointing

A cursor follows your head. You look at a target, nudge your head to drive the cursor in, and hold until it locks. This measures how fast and reliably you can acquire targets in a closed loop — the head estimator only needs a rough gain (size) calibration because you correct in real time. Runs entirely in your browser; no video is uploaded.
