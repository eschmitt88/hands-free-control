Webcam gaze & head-pose calibration

This runs entirely in your browser (MediaPipe FaceLandmarker). You'll look at a sequence of dots; the app measures how accurately your gaze and head pose can point at the screen. Nothing but small feature vectors leave your machine — no video is uploaded.
